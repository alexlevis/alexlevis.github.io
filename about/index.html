About

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Statistics & Data Science department at Carnegie Mellon. Before this, I completed a PhD in Biostatistics from Harvard University, and obtained a MSc in Biostatistics and BSc in Pharmacology from McGill University. Broadly, I am interested causal inference and missing data, and typically appeal to statistical methods backed by semiparametric efficiency theory.

A main purpose of this website is to be a platform for hosting short posts about statistics, math, or other topics. I may add a dedicated research page in the future, but for now feel free to check me out on Google Scholar to see what I’ve worked on.
